## Hello

- **Android** - Android development, which was the starting point of my career.
- **iOS** - Experiences related to Swift/SwiftUI.
- **Server** - Java server development experiences.
- **Learning Logs** - The process of learning new technologies.
- **Retrospectives** - Project retrospectives and records of my growth as a developer.

### Blog Direction

I intend to write posts that can be helpful to someone, focusing on problems encountered in practice and the processes used to solve them. I will share not only technical content but also my concerns and thoughts as a developer.

I look forward to our journey together!
