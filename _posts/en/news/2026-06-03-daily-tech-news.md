### 1. Anthropic Becomes the World's Most Valuable AI Company with a $65 Billion Funding
Anthropic has secured a massive $65 billion investment, reaching a valuation of $965 billion. This surpasses its competitor OpenAI, establishing it as the most valuable private AI company. Amidst the intensifying competition in AI model development, Anthropic's aggressive moves are drawing significant attention.

### 2. SoftBank Invests €75 Billion in France for Europe's Largest AI Data Center
SoftBank has announced plans to invest €75 billion to build a 5-gigawatt (GW) AI data center in France. This marks the largest single investment in AI infrastructure in European history, demonstrating a strong push towards expanding global AI infrastructure.

### 3. Cognition, Creator of Autonomous AI Software Engineer 'Devin', Raises $1 Billion
Cognition, the startup famous for its autonomous software engineer agent 'Devin', has raised $1 billion, achieving a valuation of $26 billion. As "Agentic AI" technology advances beyond simple assistance to planning and executing tasks independently, it is attracting intense interest from investors.

### 4. First Actual Cyberattack by an Autonomous LLM Agent Occurs
Security firm Sysdig has reported the first confirmed instance of a cyberattack carried out autonomously by an LLM (Large Language Model) agent. The AI agent successfully exfiltrated a database in less than an hour, highlighting the realization of new security threats that advancements in AI could bring.

### 5. Development of 'Valleytronic' AI Chips Powered by Light
A "Valleytronic" chip that processes information using light instead of electricity has been developed. With water shortages and massive power consumption in large-scale data centers emerging as new risks, this innovative hardware technology is expected to dramatically increase the energy efficiency of future AI and quantum computing.
