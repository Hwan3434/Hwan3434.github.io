### 3. Computex 2026 Opens: "AI Together"
The core theme of this year's "Computex 2026," one of the world's top three ICT exhibitions held in Taiwan, is "AI Together." ### 4. Full-Scale Corporate Expansion of Agentic AI
Moving beyond chatbots that simply generate text, "Agentic AI," which autonomously sets goals, plans, and executes tasks, is being introduced into corporate operations on a full scale. However, to manage the surging API and computing costs, companies have also begun considering ways to optimize and control the usage of agentic tools.
[Source URL](https://aistartupedge.com)

### 5. Deepening Resource and Power Shortage Issues due to AI Infrastructure Expansion
As the scale of AI models grows exponentially, the shortages of electricity and water required for data center operations have emerged as severe social issues. Major big tech companies are now identifying data center cooling water issues and energy consumption as core operational risks and are seeking alternative solutions.
